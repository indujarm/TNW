Ooty

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a picturesque hill station in Tamil Nadu, famous for its tea gardens, serene lakes, and the charming Nilgiri Mountain Railway. Often called the "Queen of Hill Stations," it offers a refreshing escape with its cool climate and lush landscapes.

Yelagiri

Yelagiri is a serene hill station located in Tamil Nadu, known for its pleasant weather and scenic beauty. Popular attractions include Punganoor Lake, Jalagamparai Waterfalls, and Swamimalai Hill, making it an ideal spot for trekking and nature walks.

Marina Beach

Marina Beach in Chennai is one of the longest urban beaches in the world, stretching over 13 kilometers along the Bay of Bengal. It's a bustling spot for locals and tourists alike, offering stunning sunrise views, lively street food stalls, and historic landmarks.

Yercaud

Yercaud, nestled in the Shevaroy Hills of Tamil Nadu, is a charming hill station known for its coffee plantations, lush gardens, and the tranquil Yercaud Lake. The "Jewel of the South" provides a peaceful retreat with attractions like the Anna Park and Killiyur Falls.

Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal, known as the "Princess of Hill Stations," is a beloved destination in Tamil Nadu, offering breathtaking views, mist-covered cliffs, and lush greenery. Key attractions include the serene Kodaikanal Lake, the vibrant Bryant Park, and the stunning Coaker's Walk.
