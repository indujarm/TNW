Paruppu Payasam

Paruppu Payasam is a traditional South Indian dessert made from moong dal, jaggery, and coconut milk. This sweet, creamy dish is often flavored with cardamom and garnished with fried cashews and raisins. It's a popular treat during festivals and special occasions.

Puliyodarai

Puliyodarai, also known as tamarind rice, is a flavorful South Indian dish made with cooked rice, tamarind paste, and a mix of spices. It's often garnished with peanuts and curry leaves, providing a tangy and aromatic experience. Puliyodarai is commonly served as a part of temple offerings and festive meals.

Chicken Chettinadu

Chicken Chettinadu is a spicy and aromatic dish from the Chettinad region of Tamil Nadu. This flavorful curry is made with a blend of freshly ground spices, coconut, and curry leaves, giving it a rich and robust taste. It's typically enjoyed with rice or parathas.

Mutton Kola Urundai

Mutton Kola Urundai are delicious meatballs made from minced mutton, spices, and herbs, hailing from the Chettinad cuisine. These deep-fried balls are crispy on the outside and juicy inside, often served as an appetizer or snack. They are a popular delicacy in Tamil Nadu.

Uttapam

Uttapam is a savory South Indian pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter. Unlike dosa, it is thicker and topped with various ingredients like onions, tomatoes, and green chilies. Uttapam is typically served with chutneys and sambar, making it a popular breakfast item.
